Piano

The piano is a large instrument which is played by pressing the keys on a keyboard. It was invented in the 1700s. Did you know that the piano is both a percusssion and string instrument? It also has pedals.

Violin

The violin is a string instrument which is played with a bow. It was invented in the 1550s. The violin is a fragile instrument and also one of the hardest instruments to learn.

Trumpet

The trumpet is a brass instrument which is played by pressing the valves and blowing in air. It has been around since 1500 BC. Royal castles had trumpeteers at their front doors.

Xylophone

The xylophone is a percussion instrument which is played with two mallets. It has been around since the 9th century. Many xylophones have a couple layers of bars.
